Registration for 2010 Workshop on Scheme and Functional Programming

Registration is open until the workshop begins, August 21, 2010. The normal registration fee is $65 USD, and students get a $20 USD rebate. Students must show a student ID card or other proof of being a student to the organizers at the workshop registration desk on-site. A late registration penalty of $20 USD will be charged for registrations received after 23:59 UTC, Monday August 9, 2010.

Online registration is managed by PayPal. Major credit cards as well as PayPal are acceptable forms of payment. Cash payment is only accepted at the workshop registration desk on-site.

Fees:

  • Normal registration fee: $65 USD ($85 USD after August 9)
  • Student registration fee: $45 USD ($65 USD after August 9)

Please select your registration type (Normal or Student) and click "Pay Now". The transaction processing system will ask for payment method (credit card or PayPal), credit card number, billing address, email address, etc. A receipt will be sent by email. Registration payment on your credit card statements will be labelled "PAYPAL*SCHEMEWORKS".

If you wish to make more than one registration, please repeat the process for each registration.
