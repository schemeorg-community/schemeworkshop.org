Notes on Running a Scheme Workshop

  • Olin Shivers and Michael Sperber both wrote up thoughts after their respective stints as program chairs.
  • The proceedings of the Scheme Workshop has, so far, been published as a technical report, typically at the program chair's host institution. This means that authors can feel free to submit to the Scheme workshop without worrying that a publication here precludes publication in another venue.
  • Here is some historical data showing the number of submissions, attendees, etc from past years.
  • Feel free to experiment with any features of the meeting that you think might make attendance more enjoyable: shorter talks, longer talks, panel sessions, demo sessions, invited speakers, gelato, etc. But, if possible, try to get your experiments into the call for papers so no one gets surprised.
  • Do not hesitate to contact any or all of the steering commitee members with any questions you might have.
