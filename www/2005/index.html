Purpose

The 2005 Scheme Workshop provides a forum for discussing experience with and future development of the Scheme programming language. The scope of the workshop includes all aspects of the design, implementation, theory, and application of Scheme. Past workshops have been held in Snowbird (2004), (2003), Pittsburgh (2002), Florence (2001), and Montréal (2000). We encourage everyone interested in Scheme to participate.
